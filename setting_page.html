Content Management

Posting, stories, reels assistance

Posting Help

To create a post, tap the + icon at the top of your screen, select Post, choose your photo or video, add a caption, and tap Share.

To edit a post, go to the post, tap the three dots in the top right, select Edit, make your changes, and tap Done.
Stories Help

To create a story, swipe right from anywhere in Feed, or tap Your Story, take or upload a photo or video, add effects if desired, and tap Send To > Your Story.

After creating a story, you can add stickers, text, or drawings by tapping the respective icons at the top of the screen.
Reels Help

To create a reel, tap the camera icon in the top left of your screen, select Reels at the bottom, record your video, add effects and audio, then share.

When creating a reel, you can add music, AR effects, adjust speed, and use the align tool for smooth transitions between clips.
Business Features

Insights, promotions

Insights Help

To access Insights, go to your profile, tap the menu icon, and select Insights. You'll see an overview of your account's performance.

Key metrics include Accounts Reached, Accounts Engaged, and Total Followers. These help you understand your audience and content performance.
Promotions Help

To create a promotion, go to your profile, select the post you want to promote, tap 'Promote', choose your goal, audience, budget, and duration, then tap 'Create Promotion'.

To track your promotion results, go to your profile, tap the menu icon, select 'Promotions', and choose the promotion you want to review. You'll see metrics like reach, profile visits, and website taps.

Account Security

Two-factor authentication, security settings

Enable Two-Factor Authentication
  1. Go to your Twitter account's "Settings and privacy" section
  2. Click on "Security and account access"
  3. Select "Security"
  4. Click on "Two-factor authentication"
  5. Choose your preferred method (Text message, Authentication app, or Security key)

For more detailed instructions, visit the Twitter Help Center.

Reset Your Password
Set Up Login Alerts

Login alerts notify you when someone logs into your account from a new device or location.

  1. Go to your Twitter account's "Settings and privacy" section
  2. Click on "Security and account access"
  3. Select "Security"
  4. Toggle on "Email me about login attempts"

For additional security, consider enabling two-factor authentication as well.

Tweet Management

Posting, scheduling, analytics

Twitter Posting Tips
  • Keep tweets concise and engaging
  • Use relevant hashtags to increase visibility
  • Include visuals like images or GIFs when possible
  • Engage with your audience by asking questions or running polls
  • Tweet consistently to maintain audience interest
How to Schedule Tweets
  1. Click on the "Compose Tweet" button
  2. Write your tweet as usual
  3. Instead of clicking "Tweet", click on the calendar icon
  4. Select the date and time you want the tweet to be posted
  5. Click "Confirm" to schedule your tweet

You can view and manage your scheduled tweets in the "Scheduled Tweets" section.

Understanding Tweet Analytics

Twitter Analytics provides insights into your tweet performance. Key metrics include:

  • Impressions: Number of times your tweet was seen
  • Engagements: Total number of times people interacted with your tweet
  • Engagement rate: Percentage of impressions that resulted in engagements
  • Link clicks, Retweets, Likes, and Replies

To access analytics, click on the graph icon below your tweet or visit the Twitter Analytics dashboard.

Advertising Help

Campaign setup, targeting, metrics

Setting Up a Twitter Ad Campaign
  1. Go to ads.twitter.com and log in to your Twitter Ads account
  2. Click "Create campaign" and choose your campaign objective
  3. Set your campaign budget and schedule
  4. Create your ad group and set targeting options
  5. Choose your ad format (Promoted Tweets, Promoted Accounts, etc.)
  6. Review and launch your campaign

For more detailed guidance, visit the Twitter Ads Help Center.

Twitter Ad Targeting Options

Twitter offers various targeting options to reach your desired audience:

  • Demographics: Age, gender, language, location
  • Interests: Topics and activities users care about
  • Behaviors: Past purchases, device usage
  • Keywords: Words and phrases users search for or use in their tweets
  • Followers: Target users similar to followers of specific accounts
  • Tailored audiences: Upload your own list of customers or website visitors

Experiment with different combinations to find what works best for your campaign.

Understanding Twitter Ad Metrics

Key metrics to track for your Twitter ad campaigns include:

  • Impressions: Number of times your ad was viewed
  • Engagements: Total interactions with your ad (clicks, retweets, likes, etc.)
  • Engagement rate: Percentage of impressions that resulted in engagements
  • Click-through rate (CTR): Percentage of impressions that resulted in clicks
  • Cost per result: Average cost for each desired action (varies by campaign objective)
  • Conversions: Number of users who completed your desired action

Regularly review these metrics in your Twitter Ads dashboard to optimize your campaigns.

Understanding LinkedIn Page Analytics

Key metrics to track for your LinkedIn Company Page:

  • Followers: Track growth and demographics
  • Impressions: How many times your content was seen
  • Engagement: Likes, comments, shares, and clicks on your posts
  • Visitor demographics: Job function, seniority, industry, etc.
  • Page views: How often your page is being visited
  • Custom button clicks: Track clicks on your call-to-action button

Use these insights to refine your content strategy and improve engagement.

Monetization

Revenue, partnerships, sponsorships

YouTube Partner Program (YPP)

To join the YPP and start earning money, you need to meet these requirements:

  • Have at least 1,000 subscribers
  • Have at least 4,000 valid public watch hours in the last 12 months
  • Comply with all YouTube monetization policies
  • Live in a country/region where the YPP is available
  • Have a linked AdSense account

Once you meet these requirements, you can apply for the YPP in YouTube Studio.

Maximizing Ad Revenue
  • Enable all ad formats for your videos
  • Create longer videos (over 10 minutes) to allow for mid-roll ads
  • Optimize your content for higher CPM niches
  • Increase your viewer retention to show more ads
  • Use end screens to promote more of your content
  • Regularly analyze your revenue reports in YouTube Studio

Remember, creating high-quality content that viewers want to watch is key to long-term ad revenue growth.

Securing Sponsorships and Partnerships
  1. Build a strong, engaged audience
  2. Create a media kit showcasing your channel stats and audience demographics
  3. Reach out to brands that align with your content and audience
  4. Negotiate fair rates for sponsored content
  5. Always disclose sponsored content to your audience
  6. Maintain authenticity in sponsored videos

Sponsorships can be a great way to diversify your income, but always prioritize your audience's trust.
